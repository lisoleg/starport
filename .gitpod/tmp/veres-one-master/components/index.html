What is Veres One?

Fit for Purpose

Veres One is a fit for purpose ledger designed to help people, organizations, and devices claim and secure their identity.

Self Sovereign

Identifiers claimed on the Veres One Ledger are yours forever. No person or organization can take them away from you without your permission.

Public and Permissionless

This is public and permissionless ledger, which means that anyone in the world can participate without asking for permission.

Developer Friendly

Integrating the ledger into your app is simple and easy for anyone familiar with typical Web Development.

Here Forever

The source code for the ledger is available to all and is licensed in a way that ensures that it remains a public good. Forever.

Always Free

Claiming your identity is free and will always be free. This is here to benefit every single person on the planet and beyond.
